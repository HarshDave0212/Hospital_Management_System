Keep Yourself Fit!

Eat a Balanced Diet
Include plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins to maintain overall health.

Exercise Regularly
Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise (like walking or jogging) most days of the week.

Get Enough Sleep
Doctors recommend 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night for proper body function and recovery.

Stay Hydrated
Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily to keep your body functioning properly.

Manage Stress
Practice relaxation techniques like deep breathing, meditation, or yoga to reduce stress levels.

Avoid Smoking & Limit Alcohol
Quit smoking and reduce alcohol consumption to lower the risk of heart disease and other illnesses.

Regular Health Checkups
Visit your doctor for routine screenings and early detection of potential health issues.
